Day-of Logistics

This page has information related to the day of the wedding itself. Read on to learn about the dress code, getting to the venue, and the homework assignment(s). Please check back around June 2023 for a more detailed schedule and menu.

Schedule

Doors open at 5 PM and festivities will continue until 11 PM. The ceremony will be followed by a cocktail hour before dinner is served. The whole night will be taking place at Brooklyn Grange, Sunset Park.

Please check back later for a more detailed schedule.

Directions to the Venue

See the Brooklyn Grange website for directions and accessibilty information for the Sunset Park location. Do not go to the Navy Park location; we will not be there.

Brooklyn Grange, Sunset Park is a short walk from the 36th street subway station, which is serviced by the N/Q/R/W (yellow) and the F/D (orange) lines.

If arriving by car, we urge you to check estimated arrival times starting around 3 PM the day of. Depending on traffic conditions, the drive from downtown Manhattan could take between 30 minutes and 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Parking at the Venue

Parking at the venue will be street parking only. There is usually street parking in the area with metered parking available under the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway (i.e., the Gowanus Expressway). Meters expire at 7 PM after which parking is free. While the venue has a parking lot in front of it, this lot closes on Fridays at 8 PM after which any vehicles remaining in the lot will be locked in until the following morning.

Dress Code

Dress up, treat yourself! Imagine what you would have worn to your senior prom (i.e., "Grad" for you Canadian blokes) if you were as wise and mature as you are now. Dresses do not need to be floor length, but plants very much should be. Think somewhere in the realm of "nice enough to impress at a high pressure work event" but "colorful enough it would feel weird at a high pressure work event".

The walkways are rocks. If you would like to wear heels to this wedding, try them out on cobblestone first and see how you do.

Bring a jacket for pictures then use it to claim your chair when dancing!

If at all possible, please wear orange.

Menu

This section will be updated 6-8 weeks before the wedding with the full menu including information on relevant allergies and dietary restrictions.

Cocktail hour will include passed canapes as well as a selection of cheeses, fruits, and vegetables. Dinner will be buffet style with beef and fish options for the main, a vegan salad, and two sides.

Homework

Postcard Guest Book

We know the thing everyone is really the most excited for is signing the wedding guest book. At this wedding, we are making you work for it.

Please bring to the wedding a postcard of your choosing with at least your names written on the back (longer messages welcome!). We will collect these postcards at the wedding and bind them into a coffee table book made up of your contributions!
